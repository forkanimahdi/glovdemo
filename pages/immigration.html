Immigration Programs & Structured Mobility

Developing and managing structured, regular immigration programs designed to respond to the real needs of European and international production systems.

Overview

Glovantis Group develops and manages regular, work-oriented immigration programs designed to respond in a structured way to the real needs of European and international production systems. We operate as a platform connecting businesses, institutions, organized economic systems, and qualified human capital.

Strategic vision

In a context characterized by growing demand for skilled labor and evolving migration policies, Glovantis interprets regular immigration as a strategic lever for economic development. Our model aims to:

  • Align labor supply and demand on an international scale
  • Support business competitiveness
  • Facilitate economic and professional integration
  • Contribute to structured cooperation models between countries

We operate in alignment with European frameworks and international cooperation programs, including the Piano Mattei.
